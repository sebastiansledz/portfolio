About project

I was hired by zetcom (Swiss technology company) in 2019 to start and maintain development of a new product - eMuseumPlus 2. A standard product that can serve existing and new clients as a showcase of their art collections online. As a creative developer I designed a very first Figma prototype of the new eMuseumPlus. This way I introduced Figma as a tool to the company and it started to be widely used by project managers, clients and developers.

Fresh and funky layout that clients love and developers understand. To gain reliability I developed a whole design system with reusable components, based on Tailwind CSS principles.

Till December 2022 I created more than 30 prototypes for variety of clients - museums, art galleries, city councils and even the European Parliament - zetcom got some big names in their portfolio. Several of my works are coded and publicly available and you may see examples on this page.
