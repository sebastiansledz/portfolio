About project

I used various techniques to create these graphics. First one, called Son of a Pablo is an acrylic 50x70cm painting, the next one - Lipsss... I made in Photoshop using my pen tablet, Some One I did entirely in Illustrator. I like to play with typography, metaphors and hidden messages. I consider it kind of a poetry. I love to try new tools. I have a few I know pretty well and a list of those to be yet explored. New cyber territories never stop growing.
