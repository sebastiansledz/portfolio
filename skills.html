Design for Web

Design of variety of digital assets to support marketing and promotion. Designs for online magazines and social media.

I designed various digital assets for companies I worked for. I created animated banners, infographics, brochures, annual reports, presentations. Those were shared internally, on social media sites, were presented to shareholders or clients. My designs were frequently published on renowned portals and magazines (Forbes, HBR, Berlingske).

I supported marketing campaigns with engaging interactive banners and cool animated intros. I created assets that support client offerings and increased their impact.

Design for Print

Design of variety of print materials flyers, brochures, rollups, walls. Design for newspapers and magazines.

To support company marketing I designed various print materials. For Flex Funding (Danish FinTech company) I created flyers and brochures, rollups, walls for company live events. I created adds printed in national (Danish) newspapers and magazines (Aktionaeren, Berlingske, Villabyerne). I got to cooperate with media houses, print shops, art directors.

It was remarkable support to marketing strategies, attracted new clients, improved brand visibility in media and on live events.
