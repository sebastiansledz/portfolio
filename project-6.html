eLearning for Potential Project

Web design / development / project management

About project

I was a designer and developer of the Online Learning Center for Potential Project. It is custom WordPress build backed with LearnDash for e-learning and BuddyBoss for social features. The new learning platform fills a demand for modern and interactive portal for key clients of the company. As of now clients like Ikea, Dutsche Telekom, Accenture, GSK, Lego, Microsoft and several others are using the portal as a part of their learning program.

Successfull implementation is giving facilitators tools to engage learners and clients a place to stay connected and focused on their grow.
